### ğŸ¯ A new, complete and educational end page

We recently redesigned our end page... although we liked its simplicity and sobriety, many of you told us that the lessons learned were not very clear.
So here's the new end page you can now use to view your carbon footprint results. What are the differences?
- More colours, more interaction, more animations: you learn better when you're having fun!
- A return to the target of 2 tonnes of CO2 / year / person (global target signed up to following COP21 in 2015)
- A stronger, more personalised link with our actions page: the best way to take action is to know your orders of magnitude and act accordingly
- New sharing features: the second best way to take action is to talk about it and share it!

What do you think? We'd love to hear from you, <a href="https://nosgestesclimat.fr/contact">via our contact page

### ğŸŒ¾ Improvements to the carbon model

The calculation of the emissions factors associated with the car has been revised in many places following lengthy discussions with our carbon experts:

- addition of the concept of the car owner
- revised calculation for electric cars, depending on battery type

We have also improved the calculations of the impact of fuel to bring them into line with the latest data available, and we have also renamed certain elements for greater clarity: in particular fish, which were not very clear according to a lot of the feedback we have received

Finally, with the help of ADEME, we've also rewritten the actions and updated the resources in the End <a href="https://nosgestesclimat.fr/actions">Actions</a>.

## Brand-new figures for the food footprint

We've improved the footprints for lunch and dinner dishes. 

Firstly, we've improved the calculation of the emission factor for the most important component of meals, meat and fish. Rather than a simple average, we now calculate an average weighted by national consumption (for example, for the "meat 1" dish, pork and chicken are no longer equally important). 

Secondly, in October there was a global update of agribalyse, which corrected some overestimated emission factors. We have updated our figures accordingly.

## French average rises to 9 tons! 

Unfortunately, this does not reflect any unexpected efforts, but an improvement in the Ministry of the Environment's calculation method. 

The good news is that the French administration is taking the matter seriously, improving its calculations and explaining the differences scientifically. 

Detailed explanations <a href="https://twitter.com/maeool/status/1453282887583866885">here</a>.

So why do we show an average of 9.8 tonnes in the simulator? Because our calculation is based on a different, complementary method to that used by the government. Find out more <a href="https://github.com/datagir/nosgestesclimat-site/issues/365#issuecomment-957321261">here</a>.

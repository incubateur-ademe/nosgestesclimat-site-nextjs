The footprint associated with each choice is based on a macro-economic approach. The idea is to give an initial estimate of the footprint of the activity in question without going into detail (in this version of the test, we don't want to ask for the exact number of golf clubs and balls). Thus, in this approach, part of the national footprint associated with the activity sector is allocated according to the choice made.

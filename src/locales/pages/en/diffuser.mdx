# Spreading Nos Gestes Climat

Nos Gestes Climat is a public service, its code is <a href="/documentation">free</a> and can be reused by anyone. 

We also urge you to share our carbon footprint simulator with others, in a variety of ways - and without requiring development skills!

Find out more about our <a href="https://drive.google.com/drive/u/0/folders/1ppvieBBNCSSkvREGIAAK44PmPNy9CRz9">ambassador distribution kit</a> and all the options available to you below.

Don't hesitate to let us know about this distribution <a href="/contact">on our contact page</a> so that we can add you to our <a href="/ambassadeurs">Gallery of ambassadors</a>.
